July 15, 2014 - Week 13 is Upon Us!

NASCAR and iRacing.com are teaming up to make Tuesdays NASCAR iRacing Night on FansChoice.tv during the 2015 racing season. Beginning Tuesday (February 24), race fans will be able to watch the world’s top sim racers in the NASCAR PEAK Antifreeze Series Powered by iRacing and the NASCAR iRacing Class B Series Open, featuring the same cars and tracks that are used in the NASCAR Xfinity Series, on FansChoice.tv on alternate Tuesdays at 9 pm eastern time.

“This is a really exciting development,” says Tony Gardner, president of iRacing.com. “With NASCAR iRacing Tuesdays, racing fans can tune-in to FansChoice.tv every Tuesday evening throughout the season, knowing the very best in sim racing will be on tap whether it’s the NASCAR PEAK Antifreeze Series or the NASCAR iRacing Class B Series.”

NASCAR iRacing Tuesdays kicks-off on FansChoice.tv at 9 pm eastern on February 24 with the opening round of the NASCAR PEAK Antifreeze Series at the digital Daytona International Speedway and on March 3 with NASCAR iRacing Class B Series Open at the virtual Las Vegas Motor Speedway.
